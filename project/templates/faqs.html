Where do I find GPX files?

While you can list trails without maps, mapped trails are far more useful to the public. We don't currently offer a smartphone app for tracking trails because A) That's not what this site is for; and B) There are already numerous apps on the market that do that for you. mapmyride, strava, Garmin Connect, ...

What if I don't have a GPX file?

If you don't have a GPX file to share, you can still place the trailhead on a map. If you later track the trail with GPX, you can edit the trail later and add a GPX file then.

How can I delete a trail?

On a trail page you own, click Edit, then look for the Delete button at the bottom of the Edit page.
